# 🛡️ GigGuard AI - Parametric Insurance for India's Gig Economy
**A Guidewire DEVTrails 2026 Submission | Phase 1: Ideation & Foundation**

## 1. Executive Summary
GigGuard AI is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect the income of delivery partners from uncontrollable external disruptions.  Our mission is to provide a financial safety net for workers facing immediate loss of daily wages due to weather or social curfews.

## 2. Target Persona: The Q-Commerce Partner
*  **Segment**: Grocery/Quick-Commerce (Zepto, Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart).
*  **Scenario**: Partners like Ramesh (Mumbai) lose 30% of their income when heavy rains or waterlogging halt deliveries.
*  **The Need**: Immediate, automated income replacement without the delay of manual claims.

##  3. The "Weekly" Financial Model
* **Structure**: Weekly Premium model (e.g., ₹25/week).
*  **Logic**: Matches the weekly payout cycle of gig workers to ensure affordability and high retention.
* **Scope**: **LOSS OF INCOME ONLY**.  We strictly exclude health, life, accidents, and vehicle repairs.

##  4. AI & Parametric Workflow
### A. Parametric Triggers
We use real-time monitoring via Weather APIs to trigger automatic payouts:
*  **Trigger 1**: Rainfall > 40mm in 1 hour.
*  **Trigger 2**: AQI > 350 (Hazardous pollution).
*  **Trigger 3**: Local Curfews or Zone Closures.

###  B. AI/ML Integration
*  **Dynamic Premium Calculation**: Using Machine Learning to adjust premiums based on hyper-local risk zones (e.g., higher risk in flood-prone areas).
*  **Fraud Detection**: Intelligent anomaly detection to validate worker location and prevent GPS spoofing or duplicate claims.

##  5. Tech Stack & Development Plan
*  **Frontend**: React Native (Mobile Platform chosen for worker accessibility).
* **Backend**: Node.js & Python.
*  **Integration**: OpenWeather API & Mock Payment Gateways.
