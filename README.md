# 🛡️ GigGuard AI - Parametric Insurance for India's Gig Economy
**A Guidewire DEVTrails 2026 Submission | Phase 1: Ideation & Foundation**

## 1. Executive Summary
[cite_start]GigGuard AI is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect the income of delivery partners from uncontrollable external disruptions[cite: 7, 79]. [cite_start]Our mission is to provide a financial safety net for workers facing immediate loss of daily wages due to weather or social curfews[cite: 80].

## 2. Target Persona: The Q-Commerce Partner
* [cite_start]**Segment**: Grocery/Quick-Commerce (Zepto, Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart)[cite: 82].
* [cite_start]**Scenario**: Partners like Ramesh (Mumbai) lose 30% of their income when heavy rains or waterlogging halt deliveries[cite: 10, 25].
* [cite_start]**The Need**: Immediate, automated income replacement without the delay of manual claims[cite: 11, 47].

## [cite_start]3. The "Weekly" Financial Model [cite: 15, 85]
* **Structure**: Weekly Premium model (e.g., ₹25/week).
* [cite_start]**Logic**: Matches the weekly payout cycle of gig workers to ensure affordability and high retention[cite: 18, 35].
* **Scope**: **LOSS OF INCOME ONLY**. [cite_start]We strictly exclude health, life, accidents, and vehicle repairs[cite: 17, 83].

## [cite_start]4. AI & Parametric Workflow [cite: 34, 44]
### A. Parametric Triggers
We use real-time monitoring via Weather APIs to trigger automatic payouts:
* [cite_start]**Trigger 1**: Rainfall > 40mm in 1 hour[cite: 25, 142].
* [cite_start]**Trigger 2**: AQI > 350 (Hazardous pollution)[cite: 25].
* [cite_start]**Trigger 3**: Local Curfews or Zone Closures[cite: 25, 80].

### [cite_start]B. AI/ML Integration [cite: 99]
* [cite_start]**Dynamic Premium Calculation**: Using Machine Learning to adjust premiums based on hyper-local risk zones (e.g., higher risk in flood-prone areas)[cite: 119].
* [cite_start]**Fraud Detection**: Intelligent anomaly detection to validate worker location and prevent GPS spoofing or duplicate claims[cite: 39, 42, 43].

## [cite_start]5. Tech Stack & Development Plan [cite: 100]
* [cite_start]**Frontend**: React Native (Mobile Platform chosen for worker accessibility)[cite: 98].
* **Backend**: Node.js & Python.
* [cite_start]**Integration**: OpenWeather API & Mock Payment Gateways[cite: 52, 59].
